YOUNIT APP - Design for quarantine
Connecting Italians stranded abroad by the Covid-19 health emergency.

YOUNIT - ITALIANI NEL MONDO

Design for
Quarantine

Politecnico di milano.
Interaction Design LAB.

2020


Application and website design

"The only virtual point of reference for Italians living abroad that allows them to create links with other compatriots in the same geographical area through the regulated exchange of information and participation in events generated around common interests."
The name Younit focuses its meaning on the concept of unity and closeness, inclusion and bond between Italians playing with the words "You" and "Unit".
A second level of reading also suggests "You 'n It" (you and Italy), highlighting a service aimed at reuniting the user with his homeland.
The pictogram represents the synthesis of three key concepts of the brand: community, friendliness, Italian spirit. All connected in the "y" of the naming.
The application includes 3 main sections: home, information and activities. The home serves as a wall for collecting the latest information and scheduled events. The information section is divided into a part of guides designed to help citizens in their life abroad and a part of forums where users can exchange useful information. The last section is that of activities and allows you to participate in online events organized by the users themselves and meet new people.
