LE MILLE E UNA NOTTE

Complex Artifact
System

Politecnico di milano.
Complex Artifact and System Lab.

2020


Motion Graphics

The goal of the laboratory was to design a coherent system of communicative artifacts that revolved around the promotion of a starter audiobook: "The Thousand and One Nights", a famous collection of short stories of Arab origins and settings. The leitmotif of the entire design laboratory was to learn how to make all the artifacts perceptible to an increasing number of people.

The following artifacts were developed:
- a book trailer, aimed at evoking the general atmosphere narrated in the collection;
- An instagram campaign, consisting of a virtual treasure hunt with clues hidden among the illustrations, that develops over a dozen pages created ad hoc;
- A sponsorship video of the Instagram campaign.

Moreover, further project tasks were undertaken, such as a backstage video and a report that included the whole design process.
