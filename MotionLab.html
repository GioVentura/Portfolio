LE MILLE E UNA NOTTE

Complex Artifact
System

Politecnico di milano.
Complex Artifact and System Lab.

2020


Motion Graphics

"The goal of the laboratory had been to design a coherent system of communicative artifacts that revolved around the promotion of a starter audiobook: "The Thousand and One Nights", a famous collection of short stories of Arab origin and setting. The leitmotif of the entire design laboratory was to learn how to make all the artifacts perceptible to an increasing number of people.

The artifacts made consist of:
- a book trailer, aimed at evoking the general atmosphere narrated in the collection;
- An instagram campaign, a sort of treasure hunt with clues hidden among the illustrations that develops over a dozen pages created ad hoc;
- A sponsorship video of the Instagram campaign.

Apart, as further project tasks, were made a backstage video and a report that included the whole design process.
