ComunicoGroup Experience
3-mounth Internship

COMUNICO GROUP

A first step

Torino


2017


Internship

That at ComunicoGroup, a communication agency in Turin, was my second experience in the working world, the first ever in the graphic-communication field.
I was young and inexperienced, but I learned a lot in a few months.
I consider it a fundamental step for my training: first of all because I learned the dynamics of working within an agency, I became familiar with the need to bring results in set times and above all I was able to test my skills until that moment.

Although the working environment was pleasant and the company itself had proposed to me to continue the collaboration, I felt within me the need to train at a higher level, and do it in a university.
This is because in-company training is certainly quick and efficient, but oriented: I would have learned the bare essentials that were needed to enter as a cog within a consolidated mechanism.
On the other hand, I felt the need to build solid foundations, investigate my creativity, find the right mindset and not simply be a former architect lent to graphics.
