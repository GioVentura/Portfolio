ComunicoGroup Experience
3-mounth Internship

COMUNICO GROUP

A first step

Torino


2017


Internship

The internship at ComunicoGroup, a communication agency in Turin, was my second professional experience, yet the first one in the graphic-communication field.
I was young and inexperienced, but I learned a lot in a few months. I consider it a fundamental step in my training: first of all because I learned the dynamics of working within an agency.
I became familiar with the need to meet target results within a set amount of time, and above all I was able to test the skills I had developed so far.

Although the working environment was pleasant and the company offered me to continue the collaboration, I felt the need to train at a higher level and to expand my knowledge by studying in a university.
This is because in-company training is certainly quick and efficient, but very oriented: I would have learned the bare minimum skills required to be part of the work chain.
On the other hand, I felt the need to build solid foundations, to investigate my creativity and to find the right mindset rather than being just a former architect lent to graphics.
