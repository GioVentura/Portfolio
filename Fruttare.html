Brand Identity

The following work is a transmediation of the fairy tale "L’amore delle tre melagrane" (The love of the three pomegranates) from Italo Calvino's collection of Italian Tales. Starting from the fairy tale to achieve an ecological brand that sells km0 fruit. The fairy tale, just from the first analysis, turned out to be an intense narrative full of meanings. First of all, its widespread knowledge throughout the Italian territory has determined the characterization and belonging to a strong national identity. Secondly, several aspects of narrative interest have emerged such as the patterns of narrative and the identification of roles, genres and thematic. From this deconstruction of the media text it was in fact possible to identify the deepest essence of the fairy tale, which consists in the generation of life through an infinite cycle of birth, death and reincarnation. This was the starting point that led to the hypothesis of a transmediation project focused on the ever-current issue of recycling raw materials. The result of the project led to the reincarnation of the fairy tale in a brand that, with the aim of orally transmitting habits and traditions, addresses its target by informing and raising awareness on the ways of reusing materials, making the theme of regeneration of life its own through packaging that can be reassembled in recyclable materials.
