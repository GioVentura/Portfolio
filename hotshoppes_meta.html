MetaProject Lab.

2018


Brand Identity

The goal of the course was to revitalize a brand that couldn't keep up with the industrial production that the company required but that survived in the mindset of the people, through the memory of its products and of its marketing campaigns. The meta-design activity planned the realization of some marketing artifacts such as the brand book, brand magazine, brand video and brand strategy, which included in a single marketing pack, define the brand’s identify and tell its story. The brand selected from my working team was “Hot Shoppes”, a glorious American-dinner chain still in the heart of millions of Americans.

The brand book is subdivided in 4 sections:
- HERITAGE (the legacy): observing the reasons to revitalize the brand;
- STRUTTURA (the structure): A double page summing up the brand’s values and characteristics;
- SENSO (the meaning): the description, supported by both written explanations and visual assets to have a deeper understanding of the brand’s values and characteristics;
- SINTESI (the conclusion): a double page presenting the visual content previously shown and analyzing its conceptual and practical coherence giving a visual summary of the brand’s identity.

For the Brand-Mag we acted as “curators”, selecting (or creating) editorial content that better represents the brand’s values. It connects stories of people, culture and social context, to define the story of the brand and it’s vision on the world.

The brand’s identity was summed-up using a BrandVideo. A voiceover connects the brand’s values with the visuals being displayed, without referring to specific products to advertise. The video has a musical background coherent with the visuals and shows the brand’s logo only at the very end of it, closing the video.

To Develop a digital marketing strategy and revitalize the brand on social medias, each group had to identify a CBI (Creative Business Idea), a statement that would sum up the brand’s identity and its positioning. HotShoppes had the CBI “Moments of Authentic Conviviality”.
